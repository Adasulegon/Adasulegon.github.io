Welcome to Adasu Legon

Welcome to ADA STUDENTS' UNION (ADASU), the official student organization for all students from Ada who are studying at the University of Ghana. We are here to support you in your academic and social journey on campus, and to connect you with other Ada students who share your culture, heritage and aspirations. ADASU also organizes various activities and events to promote Ada values, traditions and history, and to inspire the next generation of Ada leaders. Whether you are a new or returning student, we invite you to join us and be part of our vibrant and diverse community.

Our Mission

To foster a sense of community and belonging among Ada students at the University of Ghana, and to provide them with academic, social and cultural support.

Our Vision

To inspire and empower Ada students to achieve their academic goals, to celebrate their cultural heritage, and to contribute positively to the development of Ada and Ghana.
